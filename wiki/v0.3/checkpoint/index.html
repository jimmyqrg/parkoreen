v0.3

🏁 Checkpoint

Respawn markers — now optionally heal the player when touched.

Overview

Checkpoints save the player's respawn position. v0.3 adds the Heal at Checkpoints map-config option: when enabled, touching a checkpoint restores the player's full HP (requires the HP plugin).

Config options

OptionDefaultDescription
Opacity100Transparency 0–100.
Layer0Draw order.
The Heal at Checkpoints toggle is in Map Config, not on individual checkpoints.
