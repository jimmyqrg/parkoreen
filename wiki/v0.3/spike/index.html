△ Spike

Triangular lethal hazards. Unchanged from v0.2.

Overview

Spikes are lethal obstacles. When the player's hurt touchbox contacts a spike, they die.

Touchbox modes

ModeDescription
Triangle (default)Lethal area follows the triangular spike shape.
FullFull 32×32 cell is lethal.
