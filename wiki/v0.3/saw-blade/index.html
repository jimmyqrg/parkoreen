⚙ Saw Blade

A spinning circular hazard introduced in v0.3.

Overview

Saw blades (internally spinners) are 32×32 circular obstacles that rotate continuously. Touching a saw blade kills the player. The circular collision shape is equally dangerous from all sides.

Config options

OptionDefaultDescription
Color#787878Blade fill color.
Spin Speed2Rotation speed in degrees per tick. 0 = no spin.
Spin DirectionClockwiseClockwise (1) or Counter-clockwise (−1).
Opacity100Transparency 0–100.
Rotation0°Visual starting angle.
Layer0Draw order.
Setting Spin Speed to 0 creates a still circular obstacle — useful as a decorative hazard.
