v0.3

▶ Tester

Test your map without publishing it. Unchanged from v0.2.

Overview

The tester lets you play your map in-progress. Press Test in the editor toolbar to enter tester mode. Press Stop to return to editing.

Behavior

  • All physics, plugins, checkpoints, portals, and buttons are active.
  • Multi-select is cleared on entering tester mode.
