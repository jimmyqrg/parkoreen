v0.4

▶ Tester

Test your map — v0.4 adds R to respawn, FPS counter, and touchbox memory.

Overview

Press Test in the editor toolbar to enter tester mode. Press Stop to return to editing with the full undo history preserved.

New in v0.4

  • R key respawn — press R during testing to instantly respawn without exiting.
  • FPS counter — displayed in the tester HUD.
  • Touchbox memory — selected touchbox mode is remembered when toggling in and out of the tester.

Behavior

  • All physics, plugins, checkpoints, portals, buttons, coins, and bouncers are active.
  • Auto-save triggers on window unload.
