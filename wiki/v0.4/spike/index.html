v0.4

△ Spike

Triangular lethal hazards — v0.4 adds the all-spike touchbox mode.

Touchbox modes

ModeDescription
Triangle (default)Lethal area follows the spike's triangular shape.
FullFull 32×32 bounding box is lethal.
All-Spike (new in v0.4)Treats the entire cell, including space above the triangle tip, as lethal — useful for ceiling spikes.
