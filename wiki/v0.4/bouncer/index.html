⬆ Bouncer

Spring-style launch pads introduced in v0.4.

Overview

Bouncers launch the player in a set direction when contacted. They visually compress and spring back on activation. A bounce sound plays on each trigger.

Direction values

ValueLaunch direction
0Up
90Right
180Down
270Left
