🪙 Coin

Collectable currency items introduced in v0.4.

Overview

Coins are pickup items placed in the map. When the player touches a coin, it is collected, the player's coin total increases by the coin's Coin Amount, and a collection sound plays. Collected coins disappear until the map is reset.

Coin counter HUD

Enable Show Coin Counter in Map Config to display an on-screen HUD.

Activity scope

ScopeBehavior
globalCollected once across all players — disappears for everyone when any player picks it up.
playerEach player collects it independently.
