🪙 Coin

Collectable currency items that award the player a set amount when touched.

Overview

Coins are small pickup items scattered around a map. When the player walks into a coin it is collected and the player's coin total increases by the coin's Coin Amount. Collected coins disappear until the map is reset.

Coin counter HUD

When Show Coin Counter is enabled in Map Config, a coin counter is displayed on-screen during gameplay showing the player's current coin total.

Activity scope

ScopeBehavior
global The coin is collected once across all players. Once any player picks it up, it disappears for everyone.
player Each player collects the coin independently. Collecting it does not remove it for other players.
