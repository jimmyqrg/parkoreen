⬆ Bouncer

A spring pad that launches the player in a set direction when stepped on.

Overview

Bouncers are spring-style launch pads. When the player contacts a bouncer, they are immediately launched with a configurable velocity in the bouncer's set direction. Bouncers visually compress and spring back when triggered.

Direction values

Direction valueLaunch direction
0Up
90Right
180Down
270Left
