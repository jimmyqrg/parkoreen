🔺 Spike

Triangle hazards that kill the player on contact. Current version adds the all-spike touchbox mode.

Overview

Spikes are 32×32 triangle hazards. The map's Spike Touchbox setting determines the kill zone. In the current version a seventh mode, all-spike, was added.

Orientation

RotationAppearance
0° (default)Point facing up, base on bottom
90°Point facing right
180°Point facing down
270°Point facing left

Touchbox modes (set in Map Config)

  • normaldefaultTriangular hitbox that closely matches the visible spike shape.
  • fullFull 32×32 square hitbox. The most strict mode.
  • tipOnly the pointed tip kills. The most forgiving mode.
  • groundKill zone is the bottom row of pixels — designed for floor spikes.
  • flagTall narrow centered hitbox.
  • airOnly damages players coming from above (airborne contact only).
  • all-spikeNew in Current. Full-coverage mode: any contact with the spike from any direction is lethal.

Drop Hurt Only

When enabled in Map Config, spikes only deal damage if the player is falling (downward velocity) at the moment of contact.
