🧑 Player

Player physics, movement, hitboxes, and respawn logic.

Size

The player sprite is 32×32 px, matching one grid cell.

Physics defaults

PropertyDefault value
Gravity0.71 per tick
Jump force-13.2 (upward)
Move speed5 px/tick
Max jumps1

All values are overridable per-map in Map Config.

Hitboxes

BoxOffset from top-leftPurpose
Groundx: 0, y: 8, w: 32, h: 24Collision with solid surfaces (landing, pushing against walls/ceilings)
Hurtx: 4, y: 6, w: 24, h: 24Checked against spike and hazard hitboxes to determine death

Jump system

  • Max Jumps — number of jumps allowed before the player must land. Default: 1.
  • Infinite Jumps — when on, the player can jump at any time.
  • Additional Air Jump — grants one bonus jump while airborne (double-jump), spending from Max Jumps.
  • Jumping is triggered with W or Space.

Controls

KeyAction
A / DMove left / right
W / SpaceJump
RRespawn (in tester)

Death & respawn

  • The player dies when the Hurt box overlaps an active hazard, or when they fall below Die Line Y (default 1000 px).
  • On death the player respawns at the active checkpoint, or the map's spawn point if no checkpoint has been touched.

Coins

The player has a coin count. Collecting a Coin object increases their count by the coin's coinAmount. The coin counter HUD is shown when Show Coin Counter is enabled in Map Config.
