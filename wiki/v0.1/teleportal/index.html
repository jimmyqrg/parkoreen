🟣 Teleportal

Portal objects that instantly transport the player to a linked destination portal.

Overview

A teleportal (portal) transports the player to one or more linked partner teleportals when touched. Portals are linked by name: you give each portal a unique teleportalName and then list which portal names it should send the player to.

Portals emit a particle ring effect around their edges to indicate they are active.

Linking portals

  1. Place the first portal and give it a name, e.g. A.
  2. Place the second portal and give it a name, e.g. B.
  3. In portal A's config, set Send To to B.
  4. Optionally set portal B's Send To to A for two-way travel.

You can send to multiple portals by entering a comma-separated list in Send To. The player will be teleported to one of the listed destinations.

Config options

OptionDefaultDescription
Teleportal Name(required)Unique string identifier for this portal. Used in other portals' Send To field.
Send To(empty)Comma-separated list of teleportal names this portal sends the player to.
Color#9b59b6Glow / ring color of the portal.
Opacity100Transparency 1–100.
Rotation0°Visual rotation in 90° steps.
Flip HorizontalOffMirrors the portal sprite horizontally.
Layer0Draw order.
A portal with no Send To names will not teleport the player anywhere.
