▶ Tester

Preview and test your map inside the editor. Unchanged from v0.0.

Starting & stopping

Click Test Game in the editor toolbar to enter tester mode. The map is compiled and the player spawns at the spawn point. Click Stop or press Escape to return to the editor.

Controls

KeyAction
A / DMove left / right
W / SpaceJump
RRespawn at active checkpoint (or spawn point)
EscapeExit tester

Tester toolbar extras

  • InvincibilityOffPlayer cannot die from spikes.
  • Show TouchboxesOffRenders the player and spike hitboxes as colored rectangles.
  • Show FPSOffDisplays the current frames-per-second counter in the top corner.
Tester preferences (invincibility, touchboxes, FPS) persist between test sessions within the same editor session.
