🔺 Spike

Triangle-shaped hazard that kills the player on contact. Unchanged from v0.0.

Overview

Spikes are 32×32 triangle hazards. They use a map-level Spike Touchbox setting to determine when the player is killed.

Orientation

RotationTriangle points
0° (up)Base on bottom, point facing up
90°Base on left, point facing right
180°Base on top, point facing down
270°Base on right, point facing left

Touchbox modes (map-level setting)

  • normaldefaultTriangle hitbox matching the visible spike shape.
  • fullFull 32×32 square hitbox — strict mode.
  • tipOnly the very tip of the triangle kills. Forgiving mode.
  • groundPlayer dies only when touching the bottom row of pixels — for floor spikes.
  • flagTall narrow hitbox centered on the spike, useful for flag-shaped hazards.
  • airKills only when the player approaches from the air (no contact from sides/ground).

Drop Hurt Only

When Drop Hurt Only is enabled in Map Config, spikes only deal damage when the player is falling onto them from above. Running into the side of a spike will not kill the player.
