🏁 Checkpoint

Respawn markers that save the player's position when touched.

Overview

When the player touches a checkpoint, it becomes their new respawn position. On death they return to the most recently touched checkpoint. Checkpoints change color to show their state.

States

InactiveDefault color (grey/white flag)
ActiveActive color (yellow/gold flag)
