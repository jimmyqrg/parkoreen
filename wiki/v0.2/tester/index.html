▶ Tester

Test your map without publishing it.

Overview

The tester lets you play your map in-progress. Press Test in the editor toolbar to enter tester mode. Press Stop to return to editing.

Behavior

  • Physics, checkpoints, portals, plugins, and all game objects are active.
  • Object configs are read-only during testing.
  • The camera follows the player using the map's camera settings.

Exiting the tester

Clicking Stop returns to the editor. All objects return to their pre-test state (checkpoints deactivate, coins reappear).
