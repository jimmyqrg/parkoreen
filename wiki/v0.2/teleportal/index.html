v0.2

⬤ Teleportal

Linked portal pairs that teleport the player between two points. v0.2 adds improved visuals and particle effects.

Overview

Teleportals are placed in pairs and linked by a shared Portal ID. When the player enters one portal, they are transported to the partner portal. v0.2 improved the portal sprite and added entry/exit particle effects.

Config options

OptionDefaultDescription
Portal ID0Links two portals. Portals with the same ID are paired.
Color#8844ffPortal ring color.
Opacity100Transparency 0–100.
Layer0Draw order.
Each portal ID should be used by exactly two portals. Unpaired portals do nothing.
